01 About me

A curious student who'd rather build something than just read about it.

I'm currently in my 4th semester at Bennett University, pursuing Computer Science with a specialization in Cloud Computing. My CGPA sits around 8.0, but honestly, the projects I've shipped have taught me more than any exam.
